### Schema: `spx_standardized`

- **`alert_category`**: `indicator_snapshot` (Type 1) | `pattern_signal` (Type 2)
- **`alert_type`**: `fundamentals` | `bearish_reversal` | `overbought_hyperwave` | `confirmation_plus`
- **Core fields** (always populated): `raw_id`, `symbol`, `price`, `received_at`
- **Type 1 fields**: `rsi`, `macd`, `macd_signal`, `macd_hist`, `adx`, `vwap`, `bb_upper/middle/lower`
- **Type 2 fields**: `pattern_description`, `signal_direction`
- **Extensibility**: `metadata` JSON column for future per-type fields
